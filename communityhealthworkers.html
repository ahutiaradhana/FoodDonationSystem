Community Health Workers

Who are Community Health Workers?

Community Health Workers (CHWs) are individuals who work within their communities to promote health and wellness. They play a crucial role in connecting community members with healthcare services and resources.

Programs & Benefits Include:

  • Health education and awareness programs
  • Food donation system to support the community
  • Connecting community members with healthcare services
  • Providing support for preventive care

Daily Feeding Program

Despite the significant progress in India has made, a large number of people still face the risk of food insecurity. The COVID-19 pandemic has further exacerbated the challenge of hunger in our country.

In the heart of our community, we gather each day to share more than just meals. We share moments of compassion, kindness, and hope. This program is not just about providing nourishment; it's about creating a space where everyone feels valued and cared for.

Today, FIND currently feeds

On a monthly basis, we provide support to many individuals, encompassing families, children, seniors, and veterans.

Our primary goal is to ensure that residents have access to the nutrition necessary for carrying out their daily lives and feeling their best.

Through these collaborative efforts, we aim to alleviate hunger, foster well-being, and make a meaningful impact on the lives of those we serve.

Tomorrow, FIND offers

On a monthly basis, we extend support to many more individuals, including families, children, seniors, and veterans, ensuring that residents have the nutrition required for their daily lives, promoting their overall well-being.

Our outreach services extend beyond food distribution to connect residents with benefits programs that enhance financial security.

Community Health Workers adopt a holistic approach, connecting individuals and families to financial resources and assistance, thereby expanding their budgetary capacity. Through these comprehensive initiatives, we aim to address not only immediate nutritional needs but also contribute to the long-term financial well-being of the communities we serve.
